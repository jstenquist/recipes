Creamy horseradish sauce - rivals steakhouse quality. Excellent with prime rib, beef tenderloin, or steak.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 2 Tbsp prepared horseradish, drained
- 2 Tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp black pepper
- 1 Tbsp chives, finely chopped

## Instructions

1. In a small mixing bowl, stir all ingredients together.
2. Serve right away or cover and refrigerate for up to 2 weeks.

## Notes

- For stronger horseradish flavor, add more prepared horseradish to taste
- Prepared horseradish found in refrigerated section of grocery store
- Keeps well in fridge - flavors meld over time

## Serving Suggestions

- Prime rib
- Steak sandwiches
- Beef tenderloin
- Pork tenderloin
- Sushi (instead of wasabi)
- Deviled eggs

---

*Yields 3/4 cup, serves 12. Prep time: 2 minutes.*
