DASH-friendly take on Chipotle burrito bowls. Cilantro-lime rice, seasoned black beans, and smoky chicken — all the flavor with a fraction of the sodium.

**Ingredients**

*Chicken:*
* 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
* 2 tablespoons olive oil
* 1 tablespoon chili powder
* 1 teaspoon cumin
* 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
* 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
* 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
* 1/4 teaspoon cayenne
* Juice of 2 limes

*Cilantro-Lime Rice:*
* 1 1/2 cups long-grain white rice
* Juice of 1 lime
* 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

*Black Beans:*
* 2 cans (15 oz) no-salt-added black beans, drained
* 1/2 teaspoon cumin
* 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
* Juice of 1/2 lime

*Toppings:*
* 2 avocados, sliced
* 1 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered
* 1/4 cup red onion, diced
* Fresh cilantro
* Lime wedges
* Hot sauce (to taste)

**Instructions**

1. Toss chicken with olive oil, all spices, and lime juice. Marinate 30 min or grill immediately.
2. Grill or pan-sear chicken over medium-high heat, 5-6 min per side. Rest, then slice or chop.
3. Cook rice per package. Fluff with lime juice and cilantro.
4. Warm black beans in a small pot with cumin, garlic powder, and lime juice.
5. Build bowls: cilantro-lime rice, black beans, chicken, avocado, tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, lime wedge.

**Notes**

DASH-friendly: No-salt-added beans are key. Avocado provides potassium and healthy fats. Skip sour cream or use plain Greek yogurt instead. This is a meal prep champion — makes 5 lunches easily.
